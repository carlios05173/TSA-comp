Renewable Energy Costs

Solar Energy

Using solar energy through solar panels can help you reduce pollution in the world and reduce the cost of energy bill in the long run. A 2,000 square foot home would require around $20,000 to install solar panels. It has even become more affordable thanks to federal residential solar tax credit for homeowners. The solar tax credit allows taxpayers to claim 30% tax credit on cost of solar systems. Not only that, adding solar energy into your home can greatly reduce electric grid use and monthly utility bill. With enough panels and the right environments, you may even be able to not pay anything for light. If that wasn't enough adding solar grids to your home will likely increase the value of your home.

Taxes

Residential Clean Energy Credit is a credit that can be claimed if eligible through the use of solar, wind, or geothermal power generation, solar water heaters fuel cells, and battery storage. The percent you can claim depends on year of instalation in your home. For example, from 2022 to 2032, 30% with no annual limit or lifetime limit, in 2033 26%, and 2034 22% of credit available .
