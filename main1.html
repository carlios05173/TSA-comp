What does it mean to Live Green?

Living green means to take care of the planet by the way we use energy in our life. There are many ways to live green and it is important to know the advantages of doing so. WE HELP THE EARTH! Not only does it help reduce pollution, as a homeowner who practices living green, you can end up Saving Money

Why is living green important?

Our world is facing a major natural challenge, Global Warming and it's affecting all our aspects of live. Ozone layer is breaking increasing radiation, which is caused by the excess pollution caused by fossil fuels used to power our electricity. However, there is a solution and you can be a part of it by learning to take green energy practices into your home.

Green Energy

Green Energy doesn't necessarily mean alternative energy. They are very similar concepts, but they do have a difference. Green energy most typically refers to energy that can be produced with a method or source that doesn't harm the environment; while, alternative energy refers to sources that are naturally renewed such as wind, solar, and water energy.
